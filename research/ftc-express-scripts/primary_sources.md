## Suggested reading order

If you only have an hour:
1. FTC Analysis to Aid Public Comment (plain English)
2. Drug Channels post (Adam Fein's loophole flag)
3. KFF on TrumpRx (so the order's TrumpRx provisions make sense)
4. Truth on the Market (for the contrarian read)

If you have a half-day, add the actual Decision and Order PDF, the Goodwin and King & Spalding writeups, and the Caremark joint motion to withdraw.
